Hot Oil Noodles

Description

Work in progress

Ingredients

  • Noodles, ideally flat noodles
  • Any leafy greens
  • Light soy sauce
  • Dark soy sauce
  • Chinese black vinegear
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Scallion
  • Cilantro
  • Minced Garlic
  • Hot Oil

Instructions

  1. Boil noodles until al dente
  2. Blanch the leafy greens in the same pot
  3. Add the cooked noodles and greens to a heatproof bowl, along with the light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, Chinese black vinegar, crushed red pepper flakes, salt, scallion, cilantro, and minced garlic.

  5. Mix together and serve!
